How It Works

Sign up

Sign in with Twitter to begin

Ask your trivia

Your Twitter account posts trivia questions several times a day. Questions like “What is the Queen’s last name?”

Your followers respond

Anyone can respond by replying to the question. If they get the answer right they get points. Bonus points to the first person to get the right answer and for using the relevant hashtag.

Reward the sharpest

Fans compete for points on a leaderboard which shows the daily winners and a weekly carry-over champion. The highest scoring players win daily or weekly prizes.
